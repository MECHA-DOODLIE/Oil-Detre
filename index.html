About Oil Detre

Our product autonomously detects and cleans oil spills in marine environments. Using advanced sensors like GPS, ultrasonic, and cloud-based monitoring, Oil Detre navigates toward oil spills, cleans them with its disc skimmer, and sends real-time data back to the ship and shore facilities.
